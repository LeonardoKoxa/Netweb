The Challenge

Large enterprises often rely on reactive monitoring tools that only alert IT teams after issues occur. In critical sectors like finance, healthcare and telecom, this can lead to downtime costs of up to £1.8 million per hour. Hybrid environments, human error and strict regulatory requirements like GDPR, HIPAA and PCI‑DSS compound the problem.

Our Solution

Koneqtiva’s platform shifts network operations from reactive to predictive. Powered by large language models (LLMs) and agentic AI, our system enables IT teams to interact with their networks using natural language, receive early warnings and gain actionable insights—while maintaining full human control.

  • Conversational interface: ask questions and receive insights using everyday language.
  • Predictive analytics: anticipate problems before they cause downtime.
  • Actionable recommendations: get clear guidance on remediation steps.
  • Compliance aware: built with GDPR, HIPAA and PCI‑DSS in mind.
